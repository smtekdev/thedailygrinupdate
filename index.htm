About Jack Cardone

Hey, there. I’m Jack, host and creator of The
Daily Grin.
I’m a grump. Especially when I wake up in the
morning. I start thinking about all I need to do,
all the problems I need to solve and all the
horseshit going on in politics these days and I’m
immediately in a rotten mood.

Which is why I started this blog because I don’t
think I’m alone. I think we can all use a little
amusement to start the day and, as I have, turn
The Daily Grind into The Daily Grin. Or at least
start it that way.

Now, trust me, I’m no Louis C.K., Jerry Seinfeld
or Chris Rock. I don’t expect you to be rolling on
the floor in laughter. But I promise at least a
grin, maybe a smile, dare I say a chortle?
